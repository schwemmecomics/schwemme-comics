Also known as the Asian cobra, Indian cobra ... or spectacled cobra.

When it's raining outside, the glasses fog up on the inside. Hard to tell if you're the one who's being greeted. 

*Glasses-cleaning-movements are very hard to draw. Also in germany the word for spectacled cobra (Brillenschlange) is a derogatory term for people wearing glasses. *
