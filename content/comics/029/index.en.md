Innocence personified.

Frank always enjoys being told something nice. We all should take Pipilotta as an example and give him more compliments.

*For naming Pipilotta there were two names up for discussion: Pipilotta (from Pippi Longstocking) and Kiki. In the end they voted for Pipilotta.*
