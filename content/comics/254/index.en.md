What's all this then?

Insights into the learning process during the physics exam. In my study group, we encouraged each other to concentrate at least as often as we got distracted from studying by spontaneously quoting internet phenomena. 

*In this case, the 2013 web series ‘Haben wir noch Pepps?’ (Do we still have energy?). Energy, as in Speed, Uppers, Louee, Whiz, Rack, Crystal, Ice, ...*
